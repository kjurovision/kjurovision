Big Song Project 2020

Brand New Project by KCS!

It's official!

Kostia Creative Studios launch a brand-new project called 'Big Song Project'. It's first edition is planned on 15th August 2020.

What to do now? Now you need to submit your 10 participating candidates according to the rules and allocations:

  1. Any song cannot be repeated. If your song or another song from the same artist was already submitted by another participant, you would be asked to submit a replacement song. If you fail to do so in 24 hours after being notified, your spot for this song will be disqualified.
  2. You can submit only songs strictly according to allocations.
  3. You can submit songs from Eurovision exclusively to the respective category, even if they match criteria to be placed into another category.

So, the categories and quotas (for every submission) are:

  • Songs from the US - 2 songs;
  • Songs from the UK - 2 songs;
  • Songs from Ukraine - 1 song;
  • Songs from Russia - 1 song;
  • Songs from EU countries - 2 songs;
  • Songs from other countries - 1 song;
  • Songs from Eurovision - 1 song.

If you have any doubt where the particular song goes, you can always contact @kotvarv on Telegram.

The deadline is 24th July 2020, so hurry up!
