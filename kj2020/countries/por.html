PORTUGAL #POR

Information

Portugal has participated in the Eurovision Song Contest 51 times since its debut at the 1964 contest. Since then it has missed five contests (1970, 2000, 2002, 2013 and 2016). The contest is broadcast in Portugal by Rádio e Televisão de Portugal (RTP). Portugal hosted the 2018 contest in Lisbon.

In KJUrovision, country participated 5 times, winning 2017 KJUrovision with 34 points and bringing Salvador Sobral to Big Eurovision Final.

Portugal also got zero points in 2010 and 2018. Moreover, it failed to qualify to 2020 Final.
