SERBIA #SRB

Information

Serbia has participated in the Eurovision Song Contest 12 times since making its debut in 2007. Serbia previously participated as part of Yugoslavia (both the Socialist Federal Republic of Yugoslavia from 1961 to 1991 and the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia in 1992) and as Serbia and Montenegro (2004–2006). Serbia won the contest on its debut as an independent country in 2007, with "Molitva" performed by Marija Šerifović.

In KJUrovision, country participated 8 times, achieving its best result in 2012. Placing 3rd with 24 points, Željko Joksimović qualified to Big Eurovision Final.

Serbia three times ended up with no points, in 2010, 2016 and 2018.
