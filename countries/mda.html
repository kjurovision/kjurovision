MOLDOVA #MDA

Information

Moldova has participated in the Eurovision Song Contest 15 times, debuting in 2005. Moldova's debut in the contest in 2005 was successful, with Zdob și Zdub finishing sixth. In total, Moldova has reached the final ten times, failing to qualify from the semi-finals in 2008, 2014, 2015, 2016 and 2019.

In KJUrovision, country is to participate 8 times, achieving its best result in 2010 with 12 points and 10th place.

Moldova got no points twice, in 2009 and 2012, and failed to qualify to 2020 Final, gaining only one point in semi-final.
