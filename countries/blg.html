BULGARIA #BLG

Information

Bulgaria has participated in the Eurovision Song Contest 12 times since making its debut at the 2005 contest in Kiev. Bulgaria has failed to qualify for the final in eight out of twelve appearances at the contest, most narrowly in 2012, when Sofi Marinova lost out on the 10th qualifying place from the second semi-final in a tie-break with Norway.

In KJUrovision, country is to participate 4 times. It managed to qualify to 2020 Final but still waits for achieving the first point.
