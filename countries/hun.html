HUNGARY #HUN

Information

Hungary has participated in the Eurovision Song Contest 17 times since making its debut in 1994. Hungary attempted to participate in 1993 but failed to qualify from a special qualifying competition set up for seven former eastern bloc countries. Hungary's first contest in 1994 remains its most successful, with Friderika Bayer finishing in fourth place.

In KJUrovision, country is to participate 8 times, achieving its best result in 2015 with 11 points and 9th place.

It has been the only time Hungary received points.
