ROMANIA #ROU

Information

Romania has participated in the Eurovision Song Contest 20 times since its debut in 1994. Selecția Națională (English: The National Selection), a song contest that takes place every year in Romania, is used to select the country's entrant for that year's Eurovision Song Contest. Its voting system and format have changed over the years. In 1993, the year before its first appearance, Romania attempted to debut in the contest, but came last in the pre-qualifying round.

In KJUrovision, country is to participate 9 times, winning the contest in 2010 with 27 points. Thus, Paula Seling and Ovi qualified to Big Eurovision Final.

However, Romania got no points in 2009 and 2012.
