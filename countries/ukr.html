UKRAINE #UKR

Information

Ukraine has participated in the Eurovision Song Contest 15 times since making its debut in 2003. They first won the contest in 2004 with "Wild Dances" by Ruslana. Ukraine hosted the 2005 and 2017 contests in Kiev. Since the introduction of the semifinal round in 2004, Ukraine and Australia are the only countries outside the Big 5 to have qualified for the final of every Eurovision they have competed in (Ukraine were absent in 2015 and 2019).

In KJUrovision, country is to participate 10 times. Jamala won 2016 edition with maximum possible 34 points and qualified to Big Eurovision Final.

Ukraine received 0 points in 2012.
