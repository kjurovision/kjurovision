BOSNIA AND HERZEGOVINA #BIH

Information

Bosnia and Herzegovina has participated in the Eurovision Song Contest 19 times since making its debut in 1993, after coming second in the qualification round "Kvalifikacija za Millstreet". Prior to 1993, Bosnia and Herzegovina participated in the Eurovision Song Contest as part of Yugoslavia. Bosnia and Herzegovina returned to the contest for the first time since 2012 at the 2016 contest, where they failed to advance from the semi-finals for the first time. They then withdrew once again from the contest.

In KJUrovision, country participated 4 times, achieving its best result in 2011 with 9th place and 13 points. Bosnia and Herzegovina failed to qualify to Big Eurovision Final.

It received 0 points twice, in 2009 and 2012.
