GEORGIA #GEO

Information

Georgia has participated in the Eurovision Song Contest 12 times, debuting at the 2007 contest. Georgia planned to participate in 2009, but decided to withdraw after the European Broadcasting Union demanded it re-write its song which made reference to the Russian prime minister, Vladimir Putin.

In KJUrovision, country is to participate 6 times, achieving its best result in 2011 with 10 points and 13th place (the same place was achieved in 2010, but with 9 points).

Georgia never received 0 points in finals, however, failed to qualify to 2020 Final.
