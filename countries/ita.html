ITALY #ITA

Information

Italy has participated in the Eurovision Song Contest 45 times since making its debut at the first contest in 1956. It was one of the seven countries that competed at the first contest. Italy competed at the contest frequently until 1997. After a 14-year absence, the country returned to the contest in 2011. Italy has won the contest twice.

In KJUrovision, country participated 10 times, achieving its best result in 2015, with Il Volo winning the contest with 30 points after the additional round of voting. Another qualifier to Big Eurovision Final from Italy is Nina Zilli, placing 2nd in 2012.

Italy received zero points in 2011 and 2014, but ended up in top-5 in all other entries.
