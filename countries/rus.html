RUSSIAN FEDERATION #RUS

Information

Russia has participated in the Eurovision Song Contest 22 times since their debut in 1994. Russia won the 2008 contest with Dima Bilan performing the song "Believe". In 2018, they failed to qualify for the final for the first time in their history.

In KJUrovision, country is to participate 10 times, achieving its best result and first-time top-10 placing in 2013.

Russia systematically ends up with no points. It has already happened in 2010, 2011, 2014, 2019 and 2020.
