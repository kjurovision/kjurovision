LITHUANIA #LTU

Information

Lithuania has participated in the Eurovision Song Contest (known in Lithuania as Eurovizijos dainų konkursas) 20 times since its debut in 1994, where Ovidijus Vyšniauskas finished last, receiving "nul points". Lithuania withdrew from the contest, not returning until 1999. LT United's sixth place in 2006 with the song "We Are the Winners" is Lithuania's best result in the contest.

In KJUrovision, country is to participate 8 times, achieving its best result in 2016, scoring 21 point and lacking only 3 points to get to the top three entries.

Lithuania received 0 points three times, in 2011, 2013 and 2015.
