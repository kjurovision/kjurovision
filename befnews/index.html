Nadav Guedj To Be The First Confirmed Interval Act

Today after the 2014 voting, it was announced by Kostia Creative Studios, broadcaster responsible for Big Eurovision Final, that Nadav Guedj would return on the Eurovision stage as an interval act participant. However, it was not announced on which of the four shows he will appear - it is still secret information. One thing is that Golden Boy will definitely show us Tel Aviv!
